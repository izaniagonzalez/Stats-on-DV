The Effects of Domestic Violence on Children

By Izania Gonzalez

Domestic violence is often spoken of in the lense of adult survivors. Children witnessing this violence are also survivors, here we briefly explore some of the impacts on children.

More than 5 million children were exposed to intimate partner violence in one year

1 in 3 children who witness domestic violence were also child abuse victims

Children who witness incidents of domestic violence are at a greater risk of serious adult health problems including obesity, cancer, heart disease, depression, substance abuse, tobacco use and unintended pregnanices than peers who did not witness domestic violence

Children who witness intimate partner violence growing up are 3 times as likely as their peers to engage in violent behavior

Courts award sole or joint custody to fathers in 70% of custody cases, despite the perception that mothers always win custody of children
